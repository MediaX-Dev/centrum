E-waste World Expo

Date: 28th June – 29th June 2023

Location: Frankfurt, Germany

Attended By: Harsh Vardhan Agrawal

Attending the E-waste World Expo in Frankfurt was a pivotal experience for our scrap recycling company. With participation from over 200 companies specializing in electronics waste generation and recycling, the event was a must-attend for those involved in the entire electronic waste recycling supply chain.... This included recycling companies, critical raw material suppliers, electronics manufacturers and suppliers, ITAD companies, and more. The Expo showcased the latest innovations and solutions aimed at creating a circular economy and fostering sustainable supply chains for the future. The event covered a wide array of topics crucial to the industry, including collection and take-back strategies, circular electronics, raw materials, design for recycling, sorting solutions, urban mining, IT asset disposition, and recycling of industrial, commercial, and post-consumer electronics. Engaging with industry leaders and exploring cutting-edge technologies provided us with invaluable insights and opportunities to enhance our operations and contribute to a more sustainable future. The E-waste World Expo was not just a networking opportunity but a comprehensive learning experience that underscored the importance of innovation and collaboration in the realm of electronic waste recycling.
