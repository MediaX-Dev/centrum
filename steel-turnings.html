Steel turnings scrap is a type of ferrous scrap metal generated from the machining processes in various industries. These are small, curly shavings or chips of steel produced during the cutting, grinding, or drilling of steel components.

Applications: Steel turning scrap is widely used in steelmaking and foundry operations. Its small size and high surface area make it ideal for charging during the production processes, where it can be efficiently converted back into high-quality steel products.

Benefits of Using Steel Turnings Scrap:

  • Efficient Melting: The small, uniform size of steel turnings facilitates quick and efficient charging in furnaces.

  • Cost-Effective: Provides an economical source of raw material for steel producers, reducing the need for primary steel production.

  • Environmentally Friendly: Recycling steel turnings helps conserve natural resources and reduces waste, contributing to sustainable manufacturing practices.

Industry Standards:
Our steel turning scrap meets industry standards for quality and cleanliness, ensuring it is an excellent choice for steel recycling and production processes. We take great care in sorting and cleaning our turnings to provide the best possible product to our customers.
