5. Steel Turnings

Steel turnings scrap is a type of ferrous scrap metal generated from the machining processes in various industries. These are small, curly shavings or chips of steel produced during the cutting, grinding, or drilling of steel components.

Applications: Steel turning scrap is widely used in steelmaking and foundry operations. Its small size and high surface area make it ideal for charging during the production processes, where it can be efficiently converted back into high-quality steel products.

7. Tyre wire scrap (Shredded)

Shredded tyre wire scrap is a type of ferrous scrap metal recovered from recycled tyres. During the tyre recycling process, steel wires embedded in the tyres are extracted and shredded. This scrap provides a valuable source of steel for recycling purposes. the wire may contain rubber, textile, and other contaminants from the tyres. Rasper unit segregation is a crucial step in the tyre recycling process that significantly enhances the cleanliness and quality of shredded tyre wire scrap. Our materials with 5-50% contamination is suitable for Rasper cleaning units of India.
