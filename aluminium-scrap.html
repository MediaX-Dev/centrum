Aluminium scrap encompasses various types of discarded aluminium materials that are collected for recycling. These scraps are valuable due to aluminium's recyclability and the energy savings associated with recycling compared to primary aluminium production. Various grades of Aluminium available for sale from our company are as follows:-

  • 1) Zorba

  • 2) Aluminium scrap with plastic contamination

  • 3) UBC

  • 4) Tense Aluminium
