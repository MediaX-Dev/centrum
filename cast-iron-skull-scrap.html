Centrum Impex over the last 5 years has collaborated with several blast Furnaces and Cast iron foundries to collect and market their byproducts. We can source consistent grades of Cast iron skulls suitable according to the end user's requirements. We have become a trusted supplier of raw materials and have earned our name in the Indian market for such kinds of byproducts. Our pig iron skulls or cast iron skulls come with 90% more Fe content. They are also rich in Carbon Content (More than 3%). Our material consists of minimum slag resulting in strong recovery during the production process. Different types of skulls are generated from different production processes. Some of the Grades of skulls we have regular access to are mentioned below

Type 1: Pig Iron skulls or Beach Iron Skulls
Fe:92±1%, C:3.5-4.5%, S:0.08% max, P:0.12% max

Type 2: Cast iron Skulls recovered from the Foundry
Fe:88-90%, C:3.5-4%, S:0.08% max, P:0.12% max

Type 3: Cast iron Skulls with Phosphorous:
Fe:88±1%, C:3.5-4%, S:0.12% max, P:1.2% max
