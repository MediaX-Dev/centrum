Plate and Structural steel scrap (PNS), is a premium category of ferrous scrap metal widely used in steelmaking.

This high-quality scrap is composed of large, robust pieces of steel, including metal plates and structural steel elements such as beams, channels, and angles. PNS is a premium category of ferrous scrap metal widely used in steelmaking.

  • Composition: PNS mainly consists of Plates commonly used in industrial equipment, shipbuilding, and construction as well as Structural Steel such as beams, channels, and angles.

  • Thickness: Typically thick and robust, ensuring high-density material for efficient recycling. Materials are also free from non-metallic contaminants, excessive rust, and non-ferrous metals, ensuring a clean and high-quality input for steelmaking.

  • Applications: PNS scrap is highly valued in electric arc furnace (EAF) steelmaking and other steel production processes due to its high density and minimal impurities. It is ideal for producing high-quality steel with excellent mechanical properties.

  • Industry Standards: Our PNS scrap adheres to the ISRI (Institute of Scrap Recycling Industries) code 231, ensuring it meets industry standards for clean, open hearth steel plates and structural shapes. This guarantees consistency and reliability in every batch we supply.
